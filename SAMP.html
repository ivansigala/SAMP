With Jetson Nano, we achieve 50% water savings through:

  • Micro-climate optimization for reduced evaporation
  • Precision targeting of water to root zones
  • Adaptive scheduling based on growth stage and weather

Plant Health Analysis Workflow

1

Image Acquisition

Cameras capture plant images at regular intervals from multiple angles.

2

Preprocessing

Images are normalized for lighting and oriented consistently.

3

Feature Extraction

CNNs identify key features: leaf color, size, shape, and stem thickness.

4

Health Assessment

AI classifies plant health with 98% accuracy: optimal, stressed, or diseased.

5

Care Decision

System determines required actions: adjust irrigation, nutrients, or alert for intervention.
